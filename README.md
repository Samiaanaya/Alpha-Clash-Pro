## ⌨️ Alpha Clash Pro
[Live Link](https://samiaanaya.github.io/Alpha-Clash-Pro/)
**Unleash your keyboard Kung Fu!**

A fast-paced browser-based typing game where a random alphabet appears on screen and you must press the correct key as quickly as possible. Train your fingers, sharpen your reflexes, and climb your own high score!



## 🎮 Gameplay

- A random letter appears on the screen.
- Press the matching key on your keyboard before losing a life.
- Every correct key press earns you a point.
- You have **5 lives** — a wrong key press costs you one.
- The game ends when all lives are gone.
- Try to beat your own score each round!



## 🚀 How to Play

1. Open [Live](https://samiaanaya.github.io/Alpha-Clash-Pro/) in any modern browser.
2. Press **Enter** or click **Play Now** on the home screen.
3. Watch the letter displayed on screen.
4. Hit the correct key on your keyboard — **without looking at the keyboard!**
5. Keep going until your lives run out, then try again.

- ⌨️ On-screen keyboard with visual key feedback
- ❤️ Life system — 5 lives per game
